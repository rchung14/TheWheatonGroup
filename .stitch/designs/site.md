# The Wheaton Group, LLC — Site Map

> Talent acquisition firm specializing in Direct Placement and Contract Recruiting.
> Contact: will@wheaton-group.com

---

## Pages

### 1. Home (`/`)
**Purpose:** First impression. Convert visitors into leads.

**Sections:**
- **Nav** — Logo left | Home · About Us · Services · Careers · Contact right | CTA button: "Get Started"
- **Hero** — Full-width. Headline: *"Your Talent Acquisition Partner in Identifying the Right Candidate."* Subtext + two CTAs: "Our Services" (primary) · "Who We Are" (ghost)
- **About Snapshot** — 2-col split. Left: short intro copy about TWG's mission. Right: professional team photo or image. CTA: "Learn more about us →"
- **Services Preview** — 2 cards side by side:
  - Direct Placement — icon, headline, 1-sentence summary, "Learn More →"
  - Contract Recruiting — icon, headline, 1-sentence summary, "Learn More →"
- **Why TWG** — 3 stat/differentiator callouts (e.g., "90-Day Guarantee", "30+ Day Roles Filled", "Risk-Free Placement")
- **CTA Banner** — Navy background. "Ready to find the right fit?" + "Contact Us" button
- **Footer** — Logo, nav links, copyright, email

---

### 2. About Us (`/about`)
**Purpose:** Build trust. Tell the TWG story.

**Sections:**
- **Page Hero** — Navy bar. Page title: "About Us". Breadcrumb.
- **Mission Statement** — Large serif pull quote + 2–3 paragraphs on TWG's background, values, and approach
- **What Sets Us Apart** — 3–4 icon + text differentiators (e.g., personalized service, deep network, speed, guarantee)
- **Team / Leadership** — Founder or team member card(s) with name, title, short bio
- **CTA** — "Start working with us" → Contact

---

### 3. Services (`/services`)
**Purpose:** Explain offerings clearly. Drive inquiry.

**Sections:**
- **Page Hero** — Navy bar. Title: "Direct Placement and Contract Recruiting."
- **Direct Placement** — Full detail block: what it is, who it's for, how it works, 90-day guarantee callout, pricing model (success-based)
- **Contract Recruiting** — Full detail block: what it is, who it's for, hourly model, cancel anytime, surge/proposal use cases
- **Comparison Table** — Side-by-side: Direct Placement vs. Contract Recruiting (timeline, cost model, best for, guarantee)
- **Process** — 3–4 step horizontal timeline: Discovery → Sourcing → Vetting → Placement
- **CTA** — "Not sure which fits? Let's talk." → Contact

---

### 4. Careers (`/careers`)
**Purpose:** Job board for candidates. Drive applications.

**Sections:**
- **Page Hero** — Navy bar. Title: "Browse Opportunities."
- **Search + Filter Bar** — Text search | Work type filter (All / Remote / Hybrid / On-site)
- **Job Listings** — Card grid. Each card: Job Title, City, Work Type badge, "View Description →"
- **Job Detail Modal / Expand** — Full job description inline or modal
- **No Results State** — "No roles match your search. Check back soon or send us your resume."
- **Candidate CTA** — "Don't see the right role? Submit your resume." → email link or form

---

### 5. Contact (`/contact`)
**Purpose:** Generate leads. Make it easy to reach TWG.

**Sections:**
- **Page Hero** — Navy bar. Title: "Get in Touch."
- **Contact Form** — Fields: Name, Company, Email, Phone (optional), Service Interest (dropdown: Direct Placement / Contract Recruiting / Not Sure), Message, Submit button
- **Direct Contact** — Email: will@wheaton-group.com | Response time note
- **Split Layout** — Form left, contact info + brief reassurance copy right
